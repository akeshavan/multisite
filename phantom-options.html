BIRN Phantoms

  • Agar: http://www.brainmapping.org/NITP/images/Summer2012Slides/NITP%20multi-site%20fMRI%20Glover%20%28shrunk%29.pdf
    • 17.5 cm diameter sphere filled with Agar gel doped with $NiCl_2$ and poisonous $NaN_3$ (sodium azide prevents mold)
    • Agar has micro-bubbles, but QA is still fine
    • Used for: EPI Metrics
    • https://xwiki.nbirn.org:8443/xwiki/bin/view/Function-BIRN/What+is+the+agar+phantom+for+and+what+do+I+do+with+it

ACR Phantom

  • Multisite ACR Paper http://informahealthcare.com/doi/pdf/10.3109/0284186X.2011.582515
  • ACR Analysis Software (Open Source) http://www.opossumm.de/projects/ummacrqe
    • Paper for software: http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0730725X14000216#
    • Metrics:
      • Uniformity
      • SNR
      • Ghosting Ratio
      • Spatial Resolution
      • Spatial Linearity
  • 2124$ and may not come in time for our trip

  • Slightly different Polish ACR Phantom: http://www.pro-project.pl/en/magnetic-resonance-imaging/item/all/1084-pro-mri.html
    • 2850\$ for software,case and phantom
    • 2070\$ for phantom and case
    • 190mm outer diameter - will prbably fit most scanner head coils
    • Can be shipped to Milan (cheaper for them)
    • Case is heavy duty, can be taken on carry on
    • Metrics
      • Geometric Distortion
      • Spatial Resolution
      • Slice thickness and position
      • Interslice Gap
      • T1 and T2 values
      • Estimate of Image Bandwidth
      • Low Contrast Detectability
      • Image Uniformity
      • Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR)
      • Physical and Electronic Slice Offset
      • Point of reference
